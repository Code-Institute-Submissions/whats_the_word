How To Use


To verify the words in your chat, first you need to export the chat history.
You can use the export chat feature to export a copy of the chat history from an individual or group chat.

Note: This feature isnâ€™t supported in Germany.

  1. Open the individual or group chat on WhatsApp (not the web version)
  2. Tap More options > More > Export chat
  3. Choose to export without media (otherwise results might be affected)
  4. Save your chat history
  5. Upload the .txt file here

Don't forget to save your session id to consult or delete your results in the future :)
